About Us

Sun Corridor Network (SCN) is a cutting-edge fiber optic network dedicated to advancing connectivity, research, and education at gigabit speeds across Arizona. Sponsored by the Arizona Board of Regents and connecting Arizona's three state universities—ASU, UA, and NAU—SCN serves over 60 institutions statewide, including K-12 schools, community colleges, and libraries. Based in Tempe, we bridge the digital divide by providing reliable, high-performance internet that empowers communities, fosters innovation, and supports economic growth for all Arizonans.
